Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Elkhorn Golf Course & Banquets ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide when you visit our website or submit an inquiry.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, and any message you submit through our contact form. We do not collect payment or financial information through this website.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to respond to your inquiry, provide information about our services, and, if you have opted in, send relevant SMS or email communications.

SMS Communications

By providing your phone number and checking the SMS consent box on our contact form, you agree to receive text messages from Elkhorn Golf Course & Banquets regarding your inquiry. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply. You may reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe.

Mobile Data & Non-Sharing Policy

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted. All other use case categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external sites (such as elkhorngc.com and elkhornbanquet.com). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.
